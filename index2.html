Welcome Foodies. 

 Welcome to Ceylon Taste Food Recipes! Discover the authentic flavors of Sri Lanka with our collection of tantalizing recipes. From aromatic curries to mouthwatering snacks and desserts, our step-by-step guides will help you recreate the vibrant taste of Ceylon in your own kitchen. Join us on this flavorsome journey and savor the essence of Sri Lankan cuisine, one recipe at a time!

Main meals 

Experience the essence of Sri Lankan cuisine with our traditional main meals. From breakfast favorites like string hoppers and coconut sambol, to fragrant rice and curry for lunch, and fiery devilled dishes for dinner, our recipes will transport you to the heart of Sri Lanka's culinary heritage. Discover the flavors that define this beautiful island nation with our collection of authentic Sri Lankan main meals.

Desserts 

Indulge in the sweet delights of Sri Lankan traditional desserts. From the luscious creaminess of Watalappam to the syrupy goodness of Kiri Pani (milk toffee) and the irresistible crunch of Kokis, our dessert recipes will take your taste buds on a delectable journey through the rich flavors and unique textures of Sri Lanka. Treat yourself to the sweet wonders of Sri Lankan traditional desserts and satisfy your cravings for a truly authentic culinary experience.

Beverages 

 Quench your thirst with Sri Lanka's traditional beverages. From the fragrant spiciness of Ceylon tea, renowned worldwide for its exceptional quality, to the refreshing coolness of king coconut water and the zingy punch of woodapple juice, our collection of traditional Sri Lankan beverages offers a delightful range of flavors. Experience the rich heritage and natural goodness of Sri Lanka through our authentic beverage recipes, perfect for soothing your senses and adding a touch of tropical paradise to your day.
